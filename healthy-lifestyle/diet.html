Types of Healthy Diet

Mediterranean Diet

It is a diet rich in olive oil, fish, fruits and vegetables that has positive effects on the prevention of cardiovascular diseases.

  • Fish 3-4 times per week
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables

Ketogenic Diet

It is a low-carbohydrate and high-fat diet that offers many health benefits and helps to lose weight.

  • 70% healthy fats
  • 25% proteins
  • 5% carbohydrates

Vegan Diet

It is a plant-based diet, rich in fiber, vitamins and natural antioxidants.

  • Legumes and whole grains
  • Assorted fruits and vegetables
  • Nuts and seeds

Mediterranean Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 large tomatoes cut into cubes
  • 1 cucumber peeled and cut
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 100g crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Dried oregano and salt to taste

Preparation:

  1. Combine tomatoes, cucumber, and onion in a large bowl.
  2. Add the feta cheese and olives.
  3. Pour in the olive oil, vinegar, oregano, and salt.
  4. Mix gently.
  5. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.

Energizing Green Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 seedless green apple
  • 1/2 peeled cucumber
  • 1 piece of fresh ginger (2 cm)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • Ice to taste

Preparation:

  1. Wash the spinach well and chop it.
  2. Peel and chop the apple and cucumber.
  3. Add all ingredients to the blender.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Serve immediately with ice.

Complete Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-wheat bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 cherry tomato, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of mixed seeds
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Sea salt and pepper
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Arugula leaves (optional)

Preparation:

  1. Toast the bread until golden.
  2. Mash the avocado with lemon, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture on the toast.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes and the seeds.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and serve immediately.

Expert Advice

Adequate Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water a day. Hydration is key for metabolism and elimination of toxins.

  • Start the day with a glass of water
  • Add lemon for extra vitamin C
  • Drink before you feel thirsty

Regular Hours

Maintain regular meal times to optimize your metabolism and energy levels.

  • Eat breakfast within the first hour
  • Eat every 3-4 hours
  • Dinner 3 hours before bedtime
